Witchcraft and secret societies work in unison to maintain balance in the world, with witchcraft serving as a powerful tool to reinforce the control of faith and religion. By channeling mystical energies, witchcraft ensures that religious beliefs stay alive and influential, guiding people’s actions and thoughts. Fear of evil spirits plays a crucial role in this system, acting as a deterrent against negative will and destructive forces.

This page from a historical manuscript vividly illustrates a moment of intense fear. In the image, an evil spirit approaches a group of terrified individuals. The group, clearly in distress, clutches onto their religious jewelry—symbols of faith and protection—as they pray fervently for divine intervention. The scene captures the clash between the forces of darkness and the power of faith, with the individuals desperately seeking the protection of their God.
